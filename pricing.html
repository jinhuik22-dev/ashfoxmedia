FAQ

Questions contractors actually ask.

Does the monthly fee include my ad budget? +

No — and it shouldn't anywhere. Your ad budget is paid directly to Google and Meta from your own accounts, so you control it and can see every dollar. Our fee covers strategy, campaign management, tracking, and reporting.

Am I locked into a contract? +

No. Ads management is month-to-month. Website builds are one-time projects. If you choose the 3-payment option, ownership still transfers at launch — the split is a convenience, not a leash.

What happens if I cancel? +

You keep everything: the website, the ad accounts, the tracking setup, the historical data, and the documentation. We built it in your accounts on purpose — leaving should be easy, which is exactly why clients stay.

Which plan is right for me? +

If your website is costing you inquiries, start there — ads pointed at a weak site waste money. If your site is solid but the phone is quiet, start with Google Ads or the Full Lead System. Not sure? Book a consultation and we'll record a free teardown of your site and lead flow before the call.

Do you work outside the Bay Area? +

Yes — ads and websites are fully remote-friendly. Production work is Bay Area local while the service is in development.
